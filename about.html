Our Story

Heritage, passion, and the art of hospitality

Since 2012

A culinary journey rooted in tradition

Lumière began as a small bistro with a big dream: to create an intimate space where every detail matters.

Founder and executive chef Clara Moreau trained in Lyon and Tokyo, blending French technique with global flavors. Today, Lumière is a destination for those who appreciate precision, warmth, and an ever‑evolving menu that honors the seasons.

Excellence

We source the finest ingredients and never compromise on quality.

Seasonality

Our menu changes with the harvest, celebrating local farms and purveyors.

Warmth

We believe great food is best enjoyed with genuine hospitality.

The Lumière Experience

From the moment you step inside, you’re greeted by soft lighting, curated art, and the scent of fresh herbs. Our open kitchen invites you to watch the artistry unfold.

We offer an award‑winning wine list, private dining options, and a chef’s tasting menu that changes weekly.
